    Frame data for attacks out of shield are calculated as follows:
  • Up-B, U-Smash can be used immediately out of shield.
  • Aerials add a 3 frame jumpsquat to the startup (can jump immediately out of shield).
  • Grabs out of shield add 4 frames to startup.
  • All other options add 11 frames needed to drop shield to startup.
Use the site to see what moves you can use to punish an opponent's move that hit your shield.
Also, you can see which moves are unpunishable or only lightly punishable by your opponent on shield.
