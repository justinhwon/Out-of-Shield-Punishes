    Frame data for attacks out of shield are calculated as follows:
  • Up-B, U-Smash can be used immediately out of shield.
  • Aerials add a 3 frame jumpsquat to the startup (can jump immediately out of shield).
  • Grabs out of shield add 4 frames to startup.
  • Dash attacks add 11 frames required to drop shield and an extra 1 frame to begin the dash.
  • All other options add 11 frames needed to drop shield to startup.
Use the site to see what moves you can use to punish an opponent's move that hit your shield.
Also, you can see which moves are unpunishable or only lightly punishable by your opponent on shield.


F.A.Q.
What is 'Rapid Finisher'?
The rapid finisher of a jab.

Aren't there some missing out of shield options?
Aerial specials and specials after dropping shield are not included,
to simplify options and due to difficulty labeling the many special moves, sorry :(

What out of shield options are included?
All aerials, smash attacks, tilts, z-airs, grab, and up specials.

Are there some missing attacks on shield?
Attacks that put you in free fall like Mario Up-B are not covered,
as you can generally use your imagination to punish :)
